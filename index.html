Where am I?

If you are reading this, chances are, you have made it to VolgaCTF 2018 Final. Congratulations!

What should I do?

Get a vulnbox, then launch it on one of your team's computers and connect it to the network via a bridged interface. Set static IP to 172.29.N.3 where N stands for your team number. Play CTF!

What is my team number?

Your team number was made available to you during the draw and is also written on the lower part of the VolgaCTF tablet on the table.
